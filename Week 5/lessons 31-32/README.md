## :point_right:Comparison Operators

  - `==` Equal
  - `!=` Not Equal

  - `===` Identical
  - `!==` Not Identical

  - `>` Larger Than
  - `>=` Larger Than Or Equal

  - `<` Smaller Than
  - `<=` Smaller Than Or Equal

  ---

## :point_right:Logical Operators

  - `!` Not
  - `&&` And
  - `||` Or
